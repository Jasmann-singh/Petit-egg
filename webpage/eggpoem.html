Egg Poems

The following are the poems on my favorite egg "Oeuf frere"

the first poem:

  • Ode to Small Egg (Anant Saini) 🥚♟️

  • In the land of laughter, where legends are born,
    Lives Anant Saini, of the chuckle and yawn.
    A heart full of gold, and kindness so big—
    Yet he's known to his friends as “the small egg,” no fig.
  • "Petit Oeufs," we say, in a fancy French flair,
    For eggs so petite, they float light in the air.
    Delicate treasures, a marvel to see,
    Like Easter came early, just for you and me.
  • But wait—on the chessboard, a twist in the plot,
    Where queens go missing, more often than not.
    With bold, brave moves (and a touch of despair),
    He blunders his queen like he just doesn't care.
  • Yet still, in defeat or victorious grin,
    He’s loyal and warm, a gem deep within.
    We jest and we tease, but always with cheer,
    For Small Egg, dear Anant, we hold you near.
  • So here’s to the egg that’s tiny, not tall,
    To the blunderer king who still stands proud and tall.
    You’re one of a kind, a friend tried and true—
    Oh, Small Egg Anant, the world’s better with you. 💛

The second Poem

  • Anant "Small Egg" Saini’s Adventure 🥚🎉

  • Anant Saini, Petit Oeufs, so tiny and neat,
    Your eggs are so small, they can’t even compete!
    They’re like little secrets, too shy to be seen,
    Just hiding in the shell, you know what I mean?
  • On the chessboard, oh, you’re quite the sight,
    You move your queen like she’s lost in the night.
    “Where’s she going?” we ask with concern,
    But hey, it’s all part of your unique turn!
  • You sacrifice her like she’s got a vacation planned,
    While we sit there confused, trying to understand.
    But somehow you still pull out a surprise,
    And we all wonder—are you playing with lies?
  • Your eggs may be small, and your moves even funnier,
    But your heart’s the biggest, no need to worry, sir!
    You’re a legend, a friend, and a joy to be around,
    Even if your queen’s always lost and never found.
  • So here’s to you, Small Egg, so quirky and bright,
    Your eggs may be small, but your spirit takes flight.
    We laugh, we joke, but you’re the best we know—
    Even if your chess game’s more “Whoa, whoa, WHOA!” 😄
